Classic Savory Deviled Eggs

Description

These classic deviled eggs are delightful. They're stuffed with a creamy blend of mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and vinegar. I like to add garlic powder and garnish them with fresh dill.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked eggs, halved
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ½ teaspoon chopped fresh dill (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 12 sprigs fresh dill (Optional)
